About SAAA

The Singapore Aircargo Agents Association — serving Singapore's air cargo industry since 1972.

Welcome from the President

On behalf of the Council and Secretariat of the Singapore Aircargo Agents Association (SAAA), a warm welcome to our website. SAAA has been the voice of Singapore's air cargo agents since our founding in 1972.

We are committed to advancing the interests of our members, developing industry talent through our training programmes, and fostering strong relationships with airlines, government agencies, and international bodies.

As air cargo plays an increasingly vital role in global supply chains, SAAA continues to champion the interests of Singapore's air freight community, ensuring our members remain at the forefront of a dynamic and evolving industry.

— SAAA Council President

Vision & Mission

Vision

To be the leading representative body for air cargo agents in Singapore, recognised for our professionalism, advocacy, and industry development.

Mission

  • Represent and advocate for member interests
  • Develop industry skills and training standards
  • Facilitate networking and business connections
  • Promote safety and compliance in air cargo
History

Our Milestones

1972

SAAA Founded

The Singapore Aircargo Agents Association was established to represent air cargo agents operating out of Singapore.

1985

Training Programme Launched

Introduction of formal training courses for air cargo professionals, setting industry standards.

1998

IATA Affiliation

Formal affiliation with IATA, strengthening SAAA's international standing and advocacy capacity.

2010

Digital Services

Launch of electronic services including online Neutral Airway Bill and digital member directory.

2022

CBTA Framework Adopted

Full adoption of the Competency-Based Training and Assessment framework for Dangerous Goods training.

2025

STC 2025 Published

Release of updated Standard Trading Conditions governing member-airline relationships.

Home/Services

Services

Essential operational services and documentation support for SAAA members.

Neutral Airway Bill

Official SAAA Neutral Airway Bills for members. Available in multiple formats to suit your operational requirements. Order online or contact the secretariat.

Bar Code Labels

IATA-compliant bar code labels for air waybills and cargo tracking. Supply chain-ready formats available for both small and bulk orders.

Permit & Certificate of Origin

Assistance with Singapore customs permits and certificates of origin. Our secretariat guides members through the documentation process efficiently.

Secretariat Facilities

SAAA secretariat facilities available for members, including meeting rooms, conference facilities, and administrative support services.

Standard Trading Conditions (STC 2025)

The SAAA Standard Trading Conditions govern the relationship between member companies and their customers. The 2025 edition includes updates to liability caps, digital documentation, and cross-border provisions.
