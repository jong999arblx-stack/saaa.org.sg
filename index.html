About SAAA

The unified voice of aviation logistics and supply chain operators across Singapore for over 50 years.

Our Mission & Purpose

The Singapore Aircargo Agents Association (SAAA) acts as an essential bridge linking industry professionals with government agencies, international civil aviation groups, and global supply chain stakeholders.

We work to ensure that Singapore maintains its position as an unshakeable, highly secure, and optimized international air cargo gateway.
